The irreducible factorizations of the polynomials $\sum_{i=0}^j x^i$ for $j$ from 0 to 10 are:  
\begin{CAS}
    vars('x')
    p = 0
    for i in range(0, 10) do
        p = p + x^i
        disp(factor(p))
    end
\end{CAS}

\parseforest{p}
\bracketset{action character = @}
\begin{forest}
    for tree={draw,font=\ttfamily}
    @\forestresult
\end{forest}
